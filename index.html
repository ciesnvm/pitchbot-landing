What is difference among different engines? +
Our engines vary in capability and specialization. The Standard engine handles routine tasks efficiently, while the Pro engine leverages advanced reasoning for complex scenarios. Enterprise customers get access to custom-tuned models optimized for their specific industry.
Why don't charge on resolution by default? +
There are three reasons for it: for a lot of cases, for examples sales, the goals is not to resolve but to screen; targeting only at resolution creates incentives for simple cases; our aim is to create human augmentation rather than replacement.
How can I end the subscription? +
You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for annual plans.
What happens after the Free quota? +
Once you exceed your free quota, you'll be prompted to upgrade to a paid plan. We offer flexible pricing tiers based on usage volume. Your data and settings are preserved for 30 days to give you time to decide.
